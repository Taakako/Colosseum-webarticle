Colosseum

The symbol of Rome

Here's the timeline of colosseum:

  • 72-Construction began under the emperor Vespasian.
  • 80-Construction finished in 80 AD under the emperor Titus.
  • 80 or 81-The inaugural games of the amphitheatre were held.
  • 217-The Colosseum was destroyed by a major fire caused by lightning.
  • 240-The Colosseum was fully repaired.
  • 435-The last match of gladiator fights.
